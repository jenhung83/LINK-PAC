ABOUT US
LINK PAC Corporation is an original equipment manufacturer that produces electronics equipment for other companies. We were founded in 1989 and acquired the national ISO 9001 : 2015 certification.
Our corporation is committed to providing high quality customized products, and are renowned for the consistency and quality of our service. We have received great reviews and earned the trust of our valued customers.
